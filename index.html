PREP Notes

PRetty, Effective, and Productive Notes


Have you while taking notes in a class produced an indecipherable document with words scattered all over? Growing up, I used to think I was the only one suffering due to my illegible handwriting. Over the years, I have realized I am not alone. Recent studies have revealed that 7 in 10 people struggle to read their own notes. Despite a widespread inability to take inefficient notes, notes are considered a powerful embodied cognition tool for information recall.

We are here to improve note taking. Help us in our mission by taking the survey.
